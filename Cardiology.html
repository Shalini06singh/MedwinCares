About the Speciality

At Medwin Division of Cardiac Surgery, our aim is to reduce the impact of cardiovascular diseases through advanced therapies for patient care. Our team of cardiac surgeons with a cumulative experience of hundreds of surgeries under the leadership of Dr Naresh Trehan has experience in complex heart surgeries including Robotic, Minimally Invasive, Heart Failure, Ventricular Restoration and Ventricular Assist Devices, Percutaneous Valve Therapies, Redo Operations, Stem Cell Therapy and Transmyocardial Laser Revascularization. We have dedicated operation theatres with sophisticated infrastructure. Medwin Cardiac Surgery team strives to offer the comprehensive advice with high technical and ethical standards to patients.

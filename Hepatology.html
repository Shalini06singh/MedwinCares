About the Speciality

The Gastroenterology Division at Medwin’s Institute of Digestive and Hepatobiliary Sciences aims to provide the best possible treatment to patients who come with advanced stage of diseases involving their digestive, liver and pancreato-biliary tracts. We have advanced facilities and carry out majority of the major endoscopy and radiology assisted procedures for gastrointestinal, liver and bilio-pancreatic disorders. The unit also comprises of a Liver Clinic for early diagnosis and treatment of liver diseases along with gastrointestinal diseases.

A word from our leaders

The Medwin Institute of Digestive & Hepatobiliary Sciences is a facility for patients with gastrointestinal, liver, pancreatic, and biliary diseases. There are multiple treatment options for a disease and to provide the best possible treatment to patients, this Institute has devised protocols where medical, surgical, and allied teams jointly decide patient treatments and management.

What we treat?

  1. Crohn’s Disease
  2. Pancreatitis
  3. Ulcerative Colitis
  4. Celiac Disease
  5. Liver cancer
  6. Gastrointestinal bleeding
